Serial commands with one character:

w - forward
a - step left
s - backward
d - step right

q - turn left
e - turn right

z - reset walk position
x - reset body reach position

r - body up
f - body down

y - body shift fwd
g - body shift left
h - body shift bkwd
j - body shift right

t - body roll left
u - body roll right
i - body pitch fwd
k - body pitch bkwd

Multibyte commands:

b<X><Y><Z><Pitch><Roll><Yaw>B - body shift, x, y and z are signed bytes, 0 are centers
m<X><Y><Turn><Speed>M - movement, x, y are signed bytes, Turn is signed byte (o - no turn), Speed - unsigned byte
g<gait>G
